Hello! It's Emily again.


Just some background information.


I am a junior at the University of Michigan studying Information with a focus in UX Design and Art & Design with a focus in Visual Communications Design. I am from the big Apple and I enjoy trying new food around New York City, but also in Ann Arbor when I am in town for college.


When I am not designing, I enjoy traveling and going for boba runs. Hence the reason why I created this website. I have been to so many bubble tea stores that I can taste the difference of the same bubble tea drink order between the stores. Bubble tea runs are a great activity to do with my friends. When we want to do something small, we always go to one of our favorite bubble tea stores. I try to drink different drinks every time I go my usual store, so I can try something new.


Bubble tea are a great way for people to try out something new. There's always new flavor, epecially during special holidays or a specific season. One chain that I feel is very experimental and gives a lot of flavor to their consumer is Quickly. There are always new flavors that I want to try. A store that I find to be very creative is Yaya. They always have the coolest name for their drinks. One drink that I found very unqiue is "Scarlet Knight". The name sounds so dreamy, yet so fierce. There's a lot of creativity in it. Not to mention, Yaya likes to tape a inspirational/motivational message on the bottom of their cups. That is always something I look forward to looking at whenever I buy a drink at the place.
